Poor Generalization by Current Deep Learning Models for Predicting Binding Affinities of Kinase Inhibitors

Joint work with Kirubakaran Palani and John Karanicolas.

Short Description

Current deep learning models provide surprisingly accurate predictions of kinase-inhibitor binding affinity. Our work investigates if this high performance is representative of advances in cheminformatic deep learning or symptomatic of larger issues in developing algorithms for such tasks.
